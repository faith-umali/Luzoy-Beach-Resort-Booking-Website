About Us

Luzoy Beach Resort is a beachfront destination located in Locloc, Bauan, Batangas, known for its calm waters and relaxing atmosphere. The resort provides different accommodation options such as open huts, cottages, and air-conditioned rooms that are suitable for both day tours and overnight stays. The place is simple, peaceful, and ideal for families, friends, and groups who want to enjoy the beach without traveling far. Guests can swim, relax, and spend quality time together while appreciating the natural surroundings of the area. Our website was created to help visitors easily access information about the resort, including room details, rates, amenities, and contact numbers. It serves as an organized and convenient way for guests to check what the resort offers before their visit.
